About

Recompound Strategy is a private investment firm focused on acquiring and holding income producing real estate and other high quality assets. All profits are reinvested to expand ownership and scale long term cash flow through continuous acquisitions and disciplined allocation.

What We Do

We acquire and hold cash flowing Real Estate that funds future acquisitions. We use leverage strategically to build equity while controlling risk and let assets pay themselves down through cash flow. Profits are reinvested into new deals to expand and diversify ownership over time.

How We Compound Capital

Recompound Strategy deploys capital across three core pillars Private Equity, Real Estate, and Quant Allocation with a single objective: Grow durable cash flow and recycle it into more ownership.

Private Equity

Control or minority stakes in cash flowing businesses where operational improvement can unlock higher free cash flow without financial engineering.

  • Focus on simple, durable business models with real customers.
  • Align incentives with operators and keep balance sheets conservative.
  • Use excess cash flow to reduce risk and fund future acquisitions.

Real Estate

Income producing residential and commercial assets in markets with resilient demand and disciplined leverage.

  • Target properties where cash flow pays for capex and debt service.
  • Improve yield through renovations, better management, and tenant quality.
  • Recycle retained cash and equity into the next property rather than distributing it.

Quant & Allocation

Rules based allocation across public markets to compound excess cash while controlling risk.

  • Use systematic rules for entries, sizing, and risk management.
  • Prioritise drawdown control and capital preservation over chasing maximum return.
  • Channel realised gains back into long term private equity and real estate holdings.

Acquisition Blueprint

01 Source Target cash flowing assets that fit strict criteria.
02 Acquire & Leverage Acquire with disciplined leverage, backed by cash flow.
03 Operate & Reinvest Improve operations and recycle free cash into more assets.
04 Compound Let scale, yield, and time do the heavy lifting.
